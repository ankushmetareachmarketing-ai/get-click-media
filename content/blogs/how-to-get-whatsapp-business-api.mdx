To get WhatsApp Business API in India, you need to: (1) have a registered business with a Meta Business Manager account, (2) complete Meta business verification with your company documents, (3) work through an authorised Meta Business Solution Provider (BSP) like Get Click Media, who registers your WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) and phone number on the WhatsApp Business Platform, and (4) get your message templates approved by Meta. Total time with Get Click Media: 3 to 5 business days.

WhatsApp Business API is not available for direct self-signup from Meta. Unlike the free WhatsApp Business App, the API requires onboarding through an authorised BSP. Get Click Media manages the entire process- you provide the documents and business details, and we handle everything else.

## Step-by-Step: How to Get WhatsApp Business API

### 1. Contact Get Click Media to Begin

Reach out to Get Click Media via our website, WhatsApp, or phone. Share your business type, use case (marketing, support, OTP, or all), and expected monthly message volume. We provide a custom onboarding plan and pricing within 24 hours.

> GCM handles everything from this point. You do not interact with Meta directly.

### 2. Create or Claim Your Meta Business Manager

Meta Business Manager (business.facebook.com) is the central hub for all Meta business assets. If you already have one for Facebook ads, we link your new WhatsApp account to it. If not, Get Click Media creates one for you. This takes 15 to 30 minutes.

> You need admin access to your Meta Business Manager. GCM will request this access to set up your WhatsApp account.

### 3. Complete Meta Business Verification

Meta requires verification of your business identity before granting WhatsApp API access. You submit: Certificate of Incorporation (or GST certificate), business website URL, and business phone number. Meta reviews and approves- typically 1 to 3 business days. Get Click Media guides you through every form field. See our full [Meta business verification](/blog/meta-business-verification) guide for the complete document breakdown.

> Businesses with an existing verified Meta Business Manager skip this step entirely- saving 1 to 3 days.

### 4. Register Your WhatsApp Business Account (WABA)

Get Click Media creates your WhatsApp Business Account under your verified Meta Business Manager. We upload your business name, logo, description, and the phone number you want to use. The phone number is registered on the WhatsApp Business Platform- it cannot be simultaneously active on WhatsApp Business App.

> If your number is currently on WhatsApp Business App, it must be deregistered first. Existing chat history is preserved on the device.

### 5. Submit and Get Message Templates Approved

Business-initiated WhatsApp messages require pre-approved templates. Get Click Media drafts your initial templates- covering your primary use cases such as OTP, order confirmation, appointment reminder, and marketing campaign. We submit them to Meta and track approval. Most templates are approved within 1 to 3 business days.

> Start with 3 to 5 essential templates. You can add more anytime after going live.

### 6. Access Platform and Send First Message

You receive access to Get Click Media's campaign dashboard and REST API credentials. Our onboarding team configures your first workflow or chatbot flow. We run a test send to verify delivery. First live message sent- you are live on WhatsApp Business API.

> GCM stays available for your first week post-launch to address any issues immediately.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I apply for WhatsApp Business API directly from Meta?**
No. WhatsApp Business API access requires going through a Meta-authorised Business Solution Provider (BSP). Businesses cannot self-register directly on the WhatsApp Business Platform without a BSP. Get Click Media is an official [Meta BSP](/whatsapp-business-api-provider-india)- we handle your registration, verification, and ongoing account management.

**How long does it take to get WhatsApp Business API in India?**
With Get Click Media managing the process, most businesses go live in 3 to 5 business days. The main variables are Meta's business verification timeline (1 to 3 days) and template approval timeline (1 to 3 days), which GCM runs in parallel to minimise total time.

**Can I use my existing WhatsApp number for the API?**
Yes- you can use an existing number, but it must first be deregistered from WhatsApp Business App or consumer WhatsApp before being registered on the API. Your existing chat history stays on the device. Customers see the same number after migration- no disruption to them.

**What documents are needed to get WhatsApp Business API in India?**
Certificate of Incorporation (or GST certificate for proprietorships), business website URL, business phone number, and a business email address. Get Click Media provides a complete documents checklist during the consultation call.

**Can I get WhatsApp Business API for free?**

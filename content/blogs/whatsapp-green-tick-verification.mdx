It confirms to customers that they're messaging a genuine, verified business — not an impersonator. The process involves Meta Business Manager verification, brand name matching, and an official business account review, and typically takes 5–7 business days once submitted correctly. ## What Is Green Tick Verification?

The green tick (also called the "verified badge") appears next to a business's name in WhatsApp chats once Meta confirms the account belongs to a well-known or officially verified brand. It sits on top of WhatsApp Business API — you cannot get a green tick on the free WhatsApp Business App.

Green tick verification builds trust by signaling to customers that:

- The account is genuinely operated by the business, not a scam or clone
- Meta has independently verified the business's identity
- Messages from this number are safe to trust and act on (payments, KYC, order confirmations)

## Eligibility Requirements

- A verified Meta Business Manager account
- A registered business name matching official documents (GST/incorporation certificate)
- An active, functioning website representing the business
- A WhatsApp Business API number already provisioned
- Brand recognition Meta can independently confirm — news mentions, app store presence, or established online footprint

> **Note:** Green tick approval is at Meta's discretion — even fully eligible businesses can be asked for additional documentation.

## How to Get WhatsApp Green Tick

1. **Set up WhatsApp Business API** — Green tick review only applies to API accounts, not the free app.
2. **Complete Meta Business Manager verification** — Submit GST certificate, incorporation certificate, and business PAN.
3. **Match your brand details** — Ensure business name, website, and Meta Business Manager profile are consistent.
4. **Submit Official Business Account request** — Apply via Meta's business verification flow.
5. **Provide supporting evidence** — Share website, app listings, press mentions, or other proof of legitimacy.
6. **Meta review and approval** — 5–7 business days on average; Get Click Media tracks status and handles follow-ups.

## Why Green Tick Requests Get Rejected

- Business name mismatch between GST documents, website, and Meta Business Manager
- Incomplete or low-quality business verification documents
- No active website or minimal online presence to confirm legitimacy
- Using a personal WhatsApp number instead of a properly provisioned API number
- Applying before completing standard Meta Business Manager verification
